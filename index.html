About ElectionsZW

Updates And Analysis

ZimbabweElections2023.co.zw is the country’s first-ever extensive online platform for INDEPENDENT, fact-checked and real time electoral content & data. This includes visualised statistics, interactive maps with constituencies, battle ground states maps, articles, videos, a Manifesto promise keeper, crowdsourced content and verified electoral related data and much much more. We aim to provide you with all you need to know about the upcoming August 23rd Polls. We aim to demystify key statistics ahead of elections and then to track the elections in real time when they're happening with the use of tech, crowdsourcing and data visualisation.
