Zimbabwean elections marred by perpetual political violence

Despite several efforts by the International community, civil society organisations and election observers for Zimbabwe to end the culture of political violence, especially during polls, the southern African country continues to experience massive political violations with the latest being the murder of an opposition Citizens Coalition for Change (CCC) activist in Riverside area, Glen Norah near Tanaka Grounds, Harare.

Zimbabwean elections continue to be marred by political violence with the violations largely characterising main political parties.
